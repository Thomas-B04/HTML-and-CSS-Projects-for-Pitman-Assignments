This is an image from the game Bloodborne, featuring the character Lady Maria, who is trapped in the Hunter's Nightmare.
Lady Maria of the Astral Clocktower is a boss introduced in the The Old Hunters DLC. Bloodborne is known for its dark and atmospheric world, challenging gameplay, and rich, if sometimes disturbing, lore.
Lady Maria is a memorable character for her elegant yet deadly combat style and her connection to the game's haunting story.

Lady Maria Stats
Attribute Value
Weapon Rakuyo
HP 14,081
Attack Style Fast, Agile
Phase 1 Lady Maria fights with her Rakuyo, alternating between transformed and untransformed modes. She may occasionally use her Evelyn pistol in both forms.
Phase 2 Lady Maria stabs herself with both blades, causing a blood explosion and coating her weapons, similar to the Chikage. Her attacks gain extended range and a bloody effect. She also introduces several new blood-based attacks.
Phase 3 Lady Maria levitates and siphons the blood around her, unleashing a large blood explosion. Her previous attacks now gain a fire property that trails behind each strike, extending their range. If not dodged correctly, you can be hit by both the attack and the trailing fire.

The Hunter's Nightmare

The Hunter's Nightmare was created following the sacrilegious actions of the old hunters and Byrgenwerth scholars at the Fishing Hamlet, where the remains of a Great One named Kos had washed ashore nearby and mutated its inhabitants. The scholars and the old hunters slaughtered and mutilated the villagers in their search for "eyes" and other secrets, and had done something sacrilegious to Kos's body that would cause the creation of the Hunter's Nightmare and the curse that would befall the hunters.

Bloodborne Atmosphere

Bloodborne's atmosphere is dark, gothic, and haunting. The game immerses players in a nightmarish world filled with twisted architecture, foggy streets, and grotesque creatures. Its Victorian-inspired environments evoke a sense of dread and mystery, while eerie sound design and chilling music heighten tension. Every corner of Yharnam and the surrounding areas feels alive with danger and secrets, creating a relentless, foreboding tone that defines the game's unique horror and immersive storytelling.
